Join the new and growing Drasi community and help shape the future of this project. There are several ways to to collaborate and contribute to Drasi:

* **Start or join a discussion**: The Drasi <a href="https://aka.ms/drasidiscord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord community</a> can be used to ask questions, provide feedback, or show what you've built with Drasi.
* **Contribute Code**: Visit our <a href="https://github.com/drasi-project" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GitHub organization</a> to help improve the Drasi platform by contributing to our codebase and documentation or build a Source or Reaction to expand the Drasi ecosystem.
