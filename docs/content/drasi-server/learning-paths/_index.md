## Tips for Success

1. **Follow the sequence** - Each path is designed to build knowledge progressively
2. **Complete the exercises** - Hands-on practice reinforces learning
3. **Take notes** - Document what works for your specific use case
4. **Ask questions** - Join [Discord](https://aka.ms/drasidiscord) if you get stuck

## After Your Path

Once you complete a learning path:

- **Explore other paths** that match your expanding role
- **Dive into [Reference](/reference/)** for detailed specifications
- **Join the [Community](/community/)** to share your experience
